LifeRun

Your village has been attacked by a ferocious dinosaur. Now it's up to you to defeat the dinosaur and save your village. Find powerful weapons along the way and solve programming questions to weaken the dinosaur. Can you become the ultimate Life Runner?

Rules

  1. Answer programming questions to weaken the dinosaur.
  2. You can skip a question by jumping over it using the arrow up key.
  3. Defeat the dinosaur to save your village and win the game.
